Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2025

Welcome to our file converter app. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app to convert files.

1. About the App

Our app allows users to convert files including:

  • Images to PDF
  • PDF to Images
  • PDF to Word
  • Word to PDF
  • PDF to PPTX
  • PPTX to PDF
  • Merge PDFs
  • Compress PDF
  • Reorder PDF
  • Extract ZIP
  • Zip Files

Users can view the converted files directly through the app and access them in their device storage.

2. What Data We Handle

  • Files uploaded by the user are sent securely to our server.
  • The server processes and converts the file.
  • The converted file is sent back and saved locally on the user’s device.
  • All files are deleted from our server immediately after processing.

We do not store, share, or analyze file contents in any way.

3. Data Security

We use secure HTTPS connections to encrypt all file transfers between the app and our server.

4. No Personal Information Collected

We do not collect any personal information like name, email, or phone number. No account is required to use the app.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.
